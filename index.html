What is habitats?

Habitats are an essential part of any survival game, and they allow players to create a space where they can thrive and survive. In the context of a small boxed world based off iridium skyblock, habitats are even more crucial as the space available for players to work with is limited.



To help players make the most of their space, habitat boosters and upgrades are available. These upgrades can include things like fly and farming boosters, which allow players to move around their habitats more easily and improve their farming capabilities. Habitat size upgrades are also available, which provide players with more space to build and expand their habitats.



Creating a comfortable and functional habitat is an important aspect of the game. In addition to the practical benefits of having a well-designed habitat, it can also provide a sense of achievement and satisfaction. Players can experiment with different designs and layouts to find what works best for them and their gameplay style.



Overall, habitats are a key feature of any survival game, and in a small boxed world like iridium skyblock, they are even more important. With the help of habitat boosters and upgrades, players can create a space where they can thrive and survive in the game.
